Kafka on the Shore

Author: Haruki Murakami

Genre: Magical Realism / Fiction

Publication Date: 2002

Availability: Available

Summary:""Kafka on the Shore" follows a 15-year-old boy named Kafka Tamura who runs away from home to escape an Oedipal curse and find his missing sister and mother. Parallel to his journey is that of Nakata, an elderly man with a mysterious past who can communicate with cats. Their stories intertwine in a surreal exploration of memory, identity, and the subconscious..
